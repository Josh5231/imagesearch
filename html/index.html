Image Search Abstraction Layer

By: Josh Sanders

To use this app:

GET: https://imagesearching.herokuapp.com/api/images/Your-Search-Term

and to paginate:

GET: https://imagesearching.herokuapp.com/api/images/Your-Search-Term?offset=Page-Number


To get the last 10 search queries:

GET: https://imagesearching.herokuapp.com/api/latest/imagesearch
